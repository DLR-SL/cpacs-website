Tool-Specific Schema Files

CPACS 3.2 will support the integration of tool-specific namespaces. A selection of associated schema files can be found below:

  • acCrash: https://www.cpacs.de/toolspecific_schemaFiles/toolspecific_acCrash.xsd

Please note that the respective tool developer is responsible for providing and maintaining a schema file. Therefore, the above list does not claim to be complete or up-to-date. If you need help creating a schema file, do not hesitate to contact the CPACS team.
