Remarks

Within the wingStringerType wing stringers are defined. The stringer are defined by referencing on the stringerStructureUID, where the shape and material settings of one single stringer is defined. In addition the orientaion and the stringer pitch have to be defined:

One stringer intersects the point at the given xsi and eta position.

Alternatively, an exlicit stringer definition can be applied if the stringers shall be tapered.
