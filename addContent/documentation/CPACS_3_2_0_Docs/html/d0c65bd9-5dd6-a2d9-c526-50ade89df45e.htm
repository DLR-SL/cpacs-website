Remarks
seats describe all seats inside the aircraft. They are – strictly speaking – also objects mounted on the floor, hence floor objects. But the specific characteristics of seats justify a differentiation between seats and other objects on the floor. Seats are seat group, for example a triple seat. Therefore they have multiple seats.
